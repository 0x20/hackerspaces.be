We use, contribute and promote free and open source software in all we do. We regularly organise talks on various topics, ranging from novice to more advanced levels. We have a hackerspace in the basement of one of the university buildings, S9 on campus _Sterre_. Here, we share our knowledge and work together on projects that our courses don't cover.
